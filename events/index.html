26/10/2013
12:00 pm - 3:00 pm
Stop Watching Us: Rally Against Mass Spying
TBD, Washington DC

Join us for a rally against mass surveillance. 

StopWatching.Us is a nonpartisan coalition including Restore the Fourth and other organizations, individuals, and companies standing in opposition to the unconstitutional mass surveillance by the National Security Agency.  We demand a full Congressional investigation of America’s surveillance programs, reform to federal surveillance law, and accountability from public officials responsible for hiding this surveillance from lawmakers and the public.

On October 26, 2013 we are taking those demands to Washington. 

Momentum is building in Congress for surveillance reform and we must keep the pressure on.

RSVP today! stopwatchingusrally.eventbrite.com

The rally is free and open to the public. RSVP to receive timely event updates or check back at StopWatching.Us to get the latest details.

Stop the Lying. Stop the spying. Defend the Constitution.
