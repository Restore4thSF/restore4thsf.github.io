Friday, September 27, 2013

Unitarian Universalists v. NSA Case Management Hearing
United States District Court, Northern District of California, San Francisco CA
12:00 pm - 3:30 pm
Other

This is a protest organized by the 99% Coalition. The government is trying to delay a lawsuit against the NSA. The plaintiffs want the case to move along to the discovery phase. We will show public interest by attending inside the court room (silent observers). We also will have a protest outside which is organized with 99% Coalition, Occupy SF.  Courtroom will be boring.

“Justice Delayed is Justice Denied” and any other protests relating to NSA surveillance.

 

Courtroom 11, 19th Floor

Saturday, September 28, 2013

Institute for the Future Co-Presence Online-Offline event
Institute for the Future, Palo Alto CA
10:00 am - 5:00 pm
Other

This is an all-day event to develop innovative new co-presence technology and strategies. Co-presence includes but is not limited to livestreaming protests. This is particularly important for the DC Rally on October 26th, because it is hard for people living in the Bay Area to attend. If good ideas are developed here, we can bring them to the organizers of the rally and make them happen!

[Note, there is a conflict with our open strategy meeting]

Wednesday, October 9, 2013

NSA Surveillance: What We Know; What to Do About It
Cato Institute, Washington DC
10:00 am - 4:30 pm

The CATO Institute is hosting an event titled “NSA Surveillance: What We Know; What to Do About It” in DC. It can be watched live at www.cato.org/live. For more information please visit www.cato.org/events/nsa-surveillance-what-we-know-what-do-about-it.

Keynote address: Senator Ron Wyden (D-OR), member of the Senate’s Select Committee on Intelligence.

Speakers include: Siobhan Gorman, Wall Street Journal; Spencer Ackerman, The Guardian; Barton Gellman, Washington Post; Jim Harper, Director of Information Policy Studies, Cato Institute; and Julian Sanchez, Research Fellow, Cato Institute.

For more information please visit www.cato.org/events/nsa-surveillance-what-we-know-what-do-about-it.
