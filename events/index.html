Friday, October 25, 2013

EFF Happy Hour
Science Club, Washington DC
7:00 pm - 9:00 pm
RT4 National

Join the Electronic Frontier Foundation staff for a drink on Friday, October 25, 2013 in Washington, D.C.! Raise a glass with EFF attorneys, technologists, and activists and discover our latest work defending your freedom online. EFF’s Speakeasy events are free, informal meetups that give you a chance to mingle with local members and meet the people behind the world’s leading digital civil liberties organization. It is also our chance to thank you, the EFF members who make this work possible.

This meetup follows a citizen lobby day on Capitol Hill organized through the Stop Watching Us campaign to combat unchecked NSA surveillance. Find out more about the the lobby day and historic Saturday rally at https://rally.stopwatching.us.

Find us all at Science Club in Dupont Circle! This is a free gathering for EFF members, rally participants, and all interested parties! Space is limited. For more information contact membership@eff.org. Attendees must be 21 or older. No-host bar. Make your reservation below.

https://supporters.eff.org/civicrm/event/info?reset=1&id=67
