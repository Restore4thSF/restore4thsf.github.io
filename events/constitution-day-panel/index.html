1)  Constitution Day Panel: “Civil Liberties and National Security in the Age of Big Data”
Date: Tuesday, September 17, 2013
Time: 12:00 PM – 02:00 PM
Location: Room 587, Humanities Building
Sponsor: History Department, College of Liberal and Creative Arts
Contact: Dr. Philip J. Dreyfus
E-mail: pdreyfus@sfsu.edu
Constitution Day will be observed at SFSU with a panel discussion on civil liberties and national security. The panelists will be Daniel Ellsberg, the former military analyst who leaked the Pentagon Papers during the Vietnam War, Matthew Cagle, attorney for the Northern California ACLU, former US Ambassador David Fischer from our Department of International Relations, and Professor Sarah Crabtree from the History Department.
