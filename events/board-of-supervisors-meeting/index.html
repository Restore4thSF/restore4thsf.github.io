2 pm at City Hall, Room 250, Board of Supervisors meeting:  San Francisco’s proposed “Due Process for All” Ordinance is scheduled for a first SF Board of Supervisor’s Hearing on September 17.  The legislation prohibits law enforcement officials from detaining individuals solely in response to immigration detainer requests issued by immigration authorities under S-COMM.

See below for more information from the Gray Panthers of SF and/or go to and/or go to:  http://www.sfbos.org/ftp/uploadedfiles/bdsupvrs/bosagendas/materials/bag091713_130764.pdf for more information about this proposed ordinance from the agenda packet provided online by the SF Board of Supervisors.
