Encryption

The most effective way to prevent the government from viewing your online communications is to encrypt them. There are fast and easy ways to start using encrypted text messaging, instant messaging and email. Currently Sudoroom is hosting cryptoparties on the second Sunday of each month in Oakland. We are working with other groups to form cryptoparties in San Francisco.

 

Some members are working on meshnets, which can be used to create internet networks which are completely protected by encryption. Alcides is starting the San Francisco-based Meshnet, he can be reached via e64.us/home/contact. He’s currently testing his meshlocal and looking for volunteers. Sudoroom is starting the Oakland meshnet and eventually they will be joined.
